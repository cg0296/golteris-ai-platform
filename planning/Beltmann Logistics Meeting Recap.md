## Summary

The conversation focused on preparing for an intro meeting with Beltmann Logistics, specifically with Jillian, who appears to be a strong internal sponsor for evaluating the product. The immediate goal is to demonstrate that the platform can reduce manual transportation workflow, especially around email-heavy quoting and project coordination.

Jillian's main interest is not a generic software pitch. She wants to see whether the system can materially reduce the amount of back-and-forth her team handles with clients and carriers. ## What Jillian Cares About

- Reducing manual email volume and repetitive follow-up work.
- Minimizing touchpoints between clients, carriers, and the internal team.
- Speeding up project quoting and operational response times.
- Handling complicated project requirements without requiring extensive manual cleanup.
- Supporting client-specific workflows rather than forcing one rigid process.

## Recommended Demo Focus

- Show an agent monitoring a mailbox and reading inbound quote/project emails.
- Show the agent extracting load details and identifying missing information.
- Show the agent drafting or sending follow-up questions automatically.
- Show quote-sheet generation from inbound request data.
- Show how lane details and truck counts can be transformed into a usable structured sheet quickly.
- If possible, show cleanup of messy raw input into only the fields the team actually needs to send to carriers.
